# PRD.md - summary-writer

## Product Overview
**Name:** summary-writer
**Purpose:** Help business professionals summarize long email threads into concise key points
**Tagline:** Turn lengthy email threads into actionable summaries in seconds

## Target Users
Business professionals who receive 50+ emails daily and need to quickly understand thread context

## Core Features
1. **Email Thread Analysis** - Parse email content and identify key topics
2. **Summary Generation** - Create bullet-point summaries with action items
3. **Sender Info Display** - Show who said what in the thread

## Success Criteria
- Summary generated in under 30 seconds
- Key action items clearly identified
- User can copy summary with one click

## Out of Scope
- Email client integration
- Automatic reply sending
- Multi-language support
